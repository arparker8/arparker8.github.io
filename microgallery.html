Photos with a Micro Lens

I bought my micro lens when I started college and was taking photography class. I found I liked to get up close to different items and the lens allowed me to get more detail. During this semester I had a project that we had to do which was to make a physical photo book with some type of concept. I decided to base mine off the beautiful details that I could find in nature, all the photos below are some of my favorite ones.
